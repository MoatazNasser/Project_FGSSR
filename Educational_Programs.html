Academic Programs

Cairo University, upon the request of the Council of Graduate Studies for Statistical Research, awards grades The following academic studies:

(1) A single diploma in one of the following major specializations:

  • Statistics
  • Demography & Biostatistics
  • Computer Science
  • Information Systems and Technology Information Science & Technology
  • Operations Research
  • Operations Management

(2) Master's degree in the following disciplines:

  • Statistics
  • Demography & Biostatistics
  • Computer Science
  • Information Systems and Technology Information Science & Technology
  • Operations Research
  • Operations Management

(3) PhD degree in the following disciplines:

  • Statistics
  • Demography & Biostatistics
  • Computer Science
  • Information Systems and Technology Information Science & Technology
  • Operations Research
  • Operations Management

Professional Programs

Professional studies are a package of postgraduate programs (diploma - masters - Ph.D. (aims to create an innovative thought that combines scientific materials and practical application, So that the student can reach the highest academic degrees with practical experience open to him horizons through which he can achieve a better future. In previous periods, when organizations needed, whether governmental or private, To consultants to solve work problems or to develop a vision to raise the level of performance has always been All eyes turned to Academic Masters Yin to do this task, and then it was discovered The gap between the theoretical perception and its implementation in reality, so refer to the executives who They lack specialized scientific study. From here, the importance of professional study emerged, and also with the great speed in the rhythm of life The breadth of the students' vision and their aspiration for everything new and practical has become difficult to lose The student spends a large part of his time in theoretical education, after which he begins to apply what he has studied in practice Until his experience is complete and he is able to present himself to the labor market as a special person It combines science and experience, and from here we can review its advantages and differences from

Academic study

  1. Saving time and cost for the student as he studies the theoretical part and its applications In the same course, and does not need training after obtaining the degree, The period of preparation for work will be short and more focused.
  2. Professional studies accept graduates of colleges and higher institutes at the diploma stage From all disciplines and is not limited to accepting the same specialty required studies, and after obtaining the diploma, continue to study until arrival PHD degree .
  3. There is the possibility of completing the study in the college, even if the degree is scientific Previous (Diploma - Masters) from another college in the same specialty or specialization Similar. In this case, the courses that the student has not been exposed to are added.
  4. Scientific degree in professional studies from the Graduate School of Research Statistical Cairo University equation without reference to the Supreme Council of Universities.

Objectives and Standards of Professional Programs

1. Professional diploma degree:

It aims to rehabilitate and raise scientific competence in the applied fields of specialization Minutes in the branches of statistics, social sciences, various development, computer sciences, and information systems Operations research, operations management, decision making, software engineering, and project management risks, crises, human resources, and statistical calculations through the study of curricula Practical and advanced process, participating in work teams to prepare studies, research and projects. applied. By the end of the diploma programme, the graduate should be able to:
  1. Effective communication of all kinds.
  2. Use of information technology to serve the development of professional practice.
  3. Self-assessment and challenge his personal educational needs.
  4. Use various sources to obtain information and knowledge.
  5. Team work, time management and team leadership in familiar professional contexts.
  6. Efficient time management and continuous self-education.

2. Professional master's degree:

It aims to develop research capabilities, scientific thinking and development in the branch, field and topic Which the student chooses from the reality of the college’s research plan in the fields of statistical and population sciences Various development, computer science, information systems, operations research, operations management, and Decision, software engineering, project management, risk and crisis management, and human resources and statistical calculations, using modern scientific techniques and methods through the study of A number of courses and an applied research through an integrated thesis. By the end of the master's program studies, the graduate should be able to understand and be familiar with:
  1. Ethical and legal principles of professional practice in the field of specialization.
  2. Fundamentals and ethics of scientific research.
  3. Conducting a research study or writing a systematic scientific study on a research problem.
  4. Risk assessment in professional practices in the field of specialization.
  5. Planning to develop performance in the field of specialization.
  6. Make professional decisions in a variety of professional contexts.

3. Professional doctorate degree:

It aims to develop independent thought and the ability to innovate and develop, and to add new knowledge in the field The branch, the field and the subject chosen by the student by following the scientific, technical and research principles and deepening the research capabilities that were developed at the master’s stage by conducting a Theoretical and applied scientific research in the various branches of statistics, population, development, computer science and systems Information, operations research, operations management, decision making, software engineering and management Projects, risks, crises, human resources and statistical accounts. By the end of the Ph.D. program, the graduate should be able to understand and be familiar with:
  1. Planning to develop performance in the field of specialization
  2. Use of technological means to serve the development of professional practice.
  3. Innovation, creativity, dialogue and debate based on evidence.
  4. Ethical and legal principles of professional practice in the field of specialization.
